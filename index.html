Can You Test It?

A simple tool to check if you can test a SCP with certain conditions based on departmental guidelines

Note that there are no SCPs that you can test with a Level-0 clearance (without supervision)

Also note, that supervision for testers isn't counted and that SD Guards CANNOT (I am too lazy to program this in yet) assist with Warning and Keneq SCPs in the HCZ.
